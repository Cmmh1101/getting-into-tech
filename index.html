It is POSSIBLE, and even better when we are NOT ALONE in the journey. That is the reason why I decided to open this space where I can share my journey, path, resources, and tips to support others while Getting Into Tech.

Now, why would you be motiveted to Get Into Tech? Tech is around us period.

Name one profession and it will need something that is realed to tech. Music, restaurants, church, medicine, chemestry, education, sports, businesses, organizations, news, just to name some. And I truly beleive that me and you can use tech in any area we want to be apart.

Learning How To Code

Tech refers to a lot of different areas and some of us will move from one to another depending on interests and needs. Web development englobes two different but related areas which are frontend (what we can see on a website) and backend development everything that goes in the background).

I’m going to focus for now on FRONTEND development as the best is to start from the beginning which is Learning how to code and the path to achieving it!
